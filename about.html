ABOUT US

We are proudly non-profit, non-corporate and non-compromised. Thousands of people
like you help us stand up for a healthy internet for all. Spark Foundation exists to alleviate
poverty by enabling the world to give. It is the largest and the most trusted giving platform
in India. It enables indiindividualsand organizations to raise and donate funds conveniently
to any cause they care about, with offerings including crowdfunding, corporate giving,
cause marketing and philanthropy. We rely on donations to carry out our mission to keep
the Web open and free. Will you give today?
